About

FemiJirani is a donation platform that connects those in need with those willing to give. It's major focus is the provision of sanitary towels to those unable to afford through the collection of contributions from willing individuals. The platform allows individuals to apply for sanitary towels as well as donors to support the mission of improving the sanitary health of the less fortunate one at a time. In the FemiJirani Community, 'jirani' refers to individuals who have applied for sanitary towels whereas 'donor' refers to individuals who have supported the mission of the community. The platform is transparent as both applications and donations are updated real time and availed for users to see. Every effort counts and building of a better community, where everybody enjoys life, is possible if each individual became selfless and practised it tirelessly.
